Lemon-Garlic Chicken & Mushroom Risotto

Description

This Risotto is creamy, zesty, and packed with flavor. The lemon and garlic add a fresh twist to the classic risotto, while the chicken and mushrooms make it hearty and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium lemon, zest and juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth, warmed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced (button, cremini, or shiitake)
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooked chicken, diced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Warm the chicken broth in a small saucepan and keep it on low heat.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and saute until softened, about 3-4 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and become golden, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the Arborio rice, letting it toast lightly in the oil for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the wine (or sub extra chicken broth) and stir until mostly absorbed.
  6. Add warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Wait until the liquid is mostly absorbed before adding the next ladle. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and tender (could take well over 20 minutes).
  7. Once rice is cooked, stir in cooked chicken, lemon zest and juice. Depending on the size of the lemon, I sometimes don't use all the juice and zest to avoid overpowering the dish. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove risotto from heat and stir in parmesan cheese and butter.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
